Where does all the money go?

------spending patterns over the past 30 years.

This is a story about where American household spend their money on from 1984 to 2016. This visualization presents an in-depth look at household expenditures on different categories like food, housing, apparel, transportation, healthcare, entertainment and education at a specific point over the last 100 years. Understanding shifting spending patterns is an important aspect of understanding economic security. Generally, the spending on food and clothes has fallen while spending on education and entertainment has gone up significantly.
