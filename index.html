Where does all the money go?

------spending patterns over the past 100 years.

This is a story about where American household spend their money on from 1900 to 2000. This visualization presents an in-depth look at household expenditures on different categories like food, housing, apparel, transportation, healthcare, entertainment and education at a specific point over the last 100 years. The spending on food and clothes has fallen while spending on education and entertainment has gone up significantly.
